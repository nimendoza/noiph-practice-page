Alvin and Berto were kidnapped by the envious chocolate contest umpire. It turns out that the umpire was also a policeman. This was evidence by the fact that he said "Pare, pulis ako." Moreover, he is also a powerful sorcerer. This is evidenced by the fact that he turned all the chocolates into kamatis. Furthermore, he is bored. This is evidenced by the fact that he forced our two chocolate eaters into a gameshow-like situation.

The final round of the gameshow involves a machine which produces a bag containing exactly kamatis every time a button is pressed. The kamatis in the bag are guaranteed to be one of the types of kamatis provided by the sponsors.

The challenge for the final round is to press the button as many times as needed such that the contestants, even without looking at the contents of the bags produced, can say with 100% certainty that the following statement is true:

At least one pair of bags have exactly the same contents.

This is because if all bags are distinct, Alvin and Berto will be forced by the umpire to eat the chocolate kamatis. Eating the chocolate kamatis will grant you the ability of being able to eat chocolate kamatis for the rest of your life. Because your life will immediately end after eating the chocolate kamatis. Because eating the chocolate kamatis results in death. And death ends your life immediately.

Moreover, Alvin and Berto need to hurry up because they are being chased by a giant kamatis made of chocolate! What is the least number of times that Alvin and Berto must press the button?
