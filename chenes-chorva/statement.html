According to the classical Philippine song, a spageti can have two states: pababa and pataas. While it is in the pababa state, its height goes down every second. On the other hand, if it is in the pataas state, its height goes up every second.

When a spageti appears, it always starts in the pababa state. At some point, after an integer number of seconds, it suddenly turns into the pataas state. After several seconds, it just suddenly disappears. The time between a spageti appears and disappears is called the spageti's lifespan.
