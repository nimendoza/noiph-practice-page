Catriona is a contestant in an international pageant. She made it to the evening gown segment, which is held on a stage that can be represented as an grid of stones atop a lava lake.

A contestant starts at the top-left corner of the stage and exits on the bottom-right. Each step consists of walking from a stone to the stone in front of it, behind it, to its left, or to its right. Walking between stones takes a certain amount of energy. In fact, walking to the stone on the th row and th column takes energy, no matter where the contestant walks from.

The organizers of the pageant have implemented a mechanism to make the walking more exciting. Some stones sink to the bottom of the lake at certain times, turning the position into lava. In particular, the stone on the th row and th column sinks down on the contestant's th step, and on every third step before and every third step after. On all other steps, the stone is on top of the lake. Initially, before making any steps, all stones are on top of the lake.

Fortunately, Catriona is fireproof, immune to lava, and can walk on liquids. Unfortunately, she is wearing a lava gown which does not blend well with the lava lake, so she must avoid stepping on lava. Catriona tries to see this situation with a silver lining, and notices this gives her more opportunities to make her signature slow-motion turn.

Because the question-and-answer comes after the evening gown segment, Catriona needs to spend as little energy as possible. She does not want to risk becoming tired and making a major major mistake. She might answer that the pageant is the most important event in the last ten years of history, and that would be poor and very sad.

Help Catriona show the world, the universe rather, that she is confidently beautiful with a heart, by computing the least amount of energy needed for her route.

It is guaranteed that there is at least one way for Catriona to make it to the exit of the stage without stepping on lava.
