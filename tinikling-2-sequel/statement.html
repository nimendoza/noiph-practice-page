Each dancer has a sequence of dance moves . They intend to start in a position with enough poles in front of them and enough at the back to be able to do all their moves in the original intended order.

However, there was a misunderstanding with the organizers and instead of starting in the middle of the dance floor, where there would have been enough poles in front and at the back, they were forced to start at the very back! This means that each dancer has all the poles in front of them and no poles behind them! This presents a problem! They will not be able to do some dance moves because there is a possibility that there will not be enough poles for them to move backward! And so, some dance moves in the sequence must be skipped!

Of course, Cleverlyn doesn't want the dancers' hard work wasted and so she needs your help in figuring out which dance moves should be skipped such that the total wow factor of the remaining dance moves is maximized. Note that dancers cannot change the order of their dance moves, they can just skip some of them.

Moreover, she demanded that the organizers add bamboo poles in front such that there will always be enough bamboo in front of the dancer. (You can safely assume that there are at least poles in front of the dancer.).
