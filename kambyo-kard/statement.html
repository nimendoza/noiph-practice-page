The Price is Precise is a game show wherein the contestant is presented with different items arranged from left to right. For today the items presented are

  1. a piece of very expensive chocolate (Php 50.00)

  2. NAT-tella jar (Php 300.00)

  3. decent earphones (Php 500.00)

  4. Kok Sakto (Php 5.00)

  5. a 50-peso bill (Php 50.00)

  6. iPhone 7G (Php 40000.00)

  7. taxi boundary (Php 1000.00)

  8. sari-sari store showcase (Php 2500.00)

  9. a hundred ten peso coins (Php 1000.00)

Jakob is our contestant for today. He does not know the prices of these items. He chooses one item to start with. For example, he chooses the NAT-tella jar (item 2). The next item he chooses must be to the right of his current item and it must then be more expensive than the most recent item he chose. Otherwise, he loses everything. Next, he chooses the decent earphones (item 3). He is still in the game since it's more expensive than NAT-tella. Then, he chooses iPhone 7G (item 6). Now, he decides to activate his Kambyo Kard, which changes the rules of the game. Instead of requiring the next item to be more expensive than the current one, it must be less expensive. Otherwise he loses. He then chooses taxi boundary (item 7), and he's still good. Now, he can choose to go home and take away all the items he got. He goes home with a total of items out of !

The limitations on the Kambyo Kard is as follows:

  • You cannot go home if you haven't played the Kambyo Kard yet. Note that you don't take home anything if you are unable to choose the go home option.

  • You must choose at least one other item after playing the Kambyo Kard. Hence, you cannot play the Kambyo Kard if your current item is the rightmost one.

  • You cannot play the Kambyo Kard immediately after the first item is chosen.

Given a set of items and their prices, what is the maximum number of items a contestant in The Price is Precise win?
