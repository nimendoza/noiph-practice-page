Tombstones are placed on top of the grave of a person. It must contain the deceased person's name, their year of birth and their year of death. The tombstone of Lito Lapida, the greatest action star of all time, was recently stolen by heartless thieves. Indeed, he was not mistaken when he claimed: saging lang ang may puso.

The citizens of his country want to replace it but nobody can quite remember which year he was born and which year he died. However, some of them vaguely remember facts on when he was born.

The people agree that he still wasn't born yet on the year of the Gear Wars, a war which was totally not about the gears at all. They also agree that he was already dead on the year Half-Life 3, his most anticipated video game, was released.

Moreover, people remember that he was born in the year when there was great paranoia about the safety of bridges. Thus the government decided to demolish all bridges which were constructed before his birth year. Furthermore, the people were sure that on the year of his death, all pairs of islands in his country are connected by a series of bridges. That is, it is possible to go from any island to any other by only taking bridges.

Since the citizens of Lito's country were not exactly good problem solvers, they decided to make one tombstone for each possibility. They then put all of these on top of his grave. The idea is that one of them will be correct anyway and everyone can go on with their lives. How many tombstones does Lito Lapida now have on his grave?
