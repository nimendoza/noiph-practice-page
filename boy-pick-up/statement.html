The Pick-Up Battle scene has a close-knit community of competitors in their craft. They would regularly hold Pick-Up battles wherein players take turns delivering poetic lines to impress their audience. The winner is then decided by the loudness of the applause and cheering from the audience. Hence, it is important to have many friends in the competition itself.

Boy Pick-Up, the long retired Pick-Up champion, has inspired young amateurs have tried to work their way up to match his glory.

The contest does not give importance to how witty or creative your poetic pick-up line is. Instead, it bases your score on how loud everyone cheers for your smooth pick-up lines. Thus, it is imperative for you to have friends who will cheer for you!

After a contestant delivers an epic pick-up line, friends of the contestant will start cheering for the contestant. Due to peer pressure, friends of those who are already cheering will start cheering too. This goes on and on until all the people not cheering are not friends with any of the people cheering. Of course, the contestant is exempt from all these peer pressure. One will never cheer for one's self. This is because doing so is like carrying one's own bench or liking your own Facebook status. It is not a very classy thing to do.

The score of a contestant is determined by the total loudness of the cheers of the crowd. Given who's friends with whom and given the loudness of each person in the premises, determine the score of each person when and if they become a contestant in the pick-up battle.
