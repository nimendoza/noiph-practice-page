It's flu season and The Penguin caught it. And so instead of attacking Gotham today, he is sending penguins to Gotham City. Upon hearing the news, citizens of Gotham thought to themselves: Bahala na si Batman! However, Batman also caught the flu.

Robin always knew that drinking Vitamin C every day would pay off. It's his time to shine! There is a particular area below the Gotham border where The Penguin's penguins can walk in. We represent this area of land using an rectangular grid.

Penguins come from the area below the bottom of the grid. They can move towards any of the four cardinal directions. They cannot move diagonally. All other areas save for the rectangular grid (and the area above it) mentioned above are impassable for them. The goal of the penguins is to reach Gotham, which is on top of the area represented by the grid.

Luckily, there were already some cells in the grid which had some bat holes. Bat holes prevent penguins from passing through because they will fall straight through the slippery hole. All that's left for Robin to do is to decide where to make extra bat holes so that the penguins cannot reach Gotham.

The terrain in each of the cells of the grid is not quite even and so the cost of building a bat hole in each of the cells may differ.

Because some parts of the map are historical landmarks, Robin is not allowed to put bat holes in some cells. These will be marked in the map.

As Batman is spending a lot of Baht for his full recovery, Robin must economically choose which cells to make bat holes in. He must choose these cells so that the total cost is minimized. Given the cost of building a bat hole in each cell of the grid, what is the minimum value of ?

Input Format

The first line of input contains a single integer , the number of test cases.

The first line of each test case contains two integers and . The next lines describe the grid. Each line will contain a combination of space-separated strings. These strings will either be an integer or an uppercase letter.

  • If the th string of the th line is an integer , it means that Robin can drill a bat hole on the th row and th column of the grid by paying Baht.
  • If the th string of the th line is the uppercase letter O, it means that there already is a bat hole on the th row and th column of the grid.
  • If the th string of the th line is any uppercase letter apart from O, it means that there already is a historical monument on the th row and th column of the grid.
