Yumamma lives in a planet where all the living creatures are rectangular-shaped. You could talk about their height and you could talk about their width! Moreover, what's peculiar about their height and width is that all creatures in the planet have integer dimensions when measured in meters. Furthermore, inhabitants of the planet of Yumamma are so fat that their width is never less than their height.

Yumamma's so huge that if she gets meters taller and meters wider, her area (remember that she is rectangle-shaped) increases by a factor of four! This property of Yumamma makes her feel like a freak! This makes Yumamma so sad. Yumamma wants to find other creatures like her. The leaders support Yumamma in her endeavor of finding other creatures which have the same property. This is because Yumamma's so old and the leaders support their elders.
