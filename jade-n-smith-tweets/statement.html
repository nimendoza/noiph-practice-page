Jade N. Smith has recently opened an account in Twitter. However, he does not know when he should capitalize words and when he should not. To be sure, he capitalizes EVERY word in his tweet. Moreover, he is not a jejemon, and so all the other letters (aside from the first one) of each word must be in lowercase.

Input Format
The input will contain a single line containing Jade N. Smith's (not necessarily capitalized) thoughts. Jade N. Smith's thoughts only consist of spaces and letters of the alphabet.

Output Format
Output one line containing Jade N. Smith's exact thoughts but with every word capitalized.

Constraints
Jade N. Smith's thoughts are at most 140 characters and consist only of spaces and letters of the alphabet.

Sample Input 

how can mirrors be real if our eyes arent rEAL

Sample Output 

How Can Mirrors Be Real If Our Eyes Arent Real
