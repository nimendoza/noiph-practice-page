The National Operatives of Informatica (NOI) have discovered a planned alien invasion on Informatica! It is up to you, brave programmer, to help the citizens of Informatica defend against this dastardly alien threat.

Through their incredible hacking skills, NOI have found the potential attack zones that the aliens plan to invade. For some strange reason unknown to us, the aliens have decided to attack rectangle-shaped zones in Informatica. Furthermore, all of those rectangles have sides that are parallel to either the south border of Informatica, or the east border of Informatica (Informatica is shaped like a big square). Some of these zones overlap with each other.

Furthermore, NOI has found that the aliens have planned two stages of attack: in the first stage, the aliens plan to attack the part of Informatica common to all of the attack zones with a devastating ion cannon. In the second stage, the aliens plan to attack every attack zone with massive nuclear bombs.

Your task is simple. You must find the area of Informatica that is under threat of the ion cannon attack, and the area of Informatica that is under threat of the nuclear bomb attack. Act with haste, for the future of Informatica is in your hands!
