John Eddie wants to join the Ninjas of Intellect, Prestige and Honor (NOIPH). It is an association which secretly prides itself in espionage. This is actually surprising because it is nowhere stated in their name. That's very sneaky, don't you think?

John Eddie is trying to practice for the sculpting test. In this test, usually held in the Sculptors Exhibition room, there is a lone sculpture on the stage.

The goal of the test is for the applicant to change the sculpture (by resculpting it) on the stage without any of the audience seeing the applicant do it. Applicants are provided an unlimited number of smoke bombs which block the view of the audience for exactly seconds. After the smoke clears, there should be a sculpture on the stage different from the sculpture before the smoke appeared. Then after the audience sees the sculpture, the applicant can throw another smoke bomb so that he can work on the next sculpture. Note that applicants are not allowed to use multiple smoke bombs to lengthen their time to change the sculpture. In other words, the audience must see a sculpture between the activation of smoke bombs.

Note that John Eddie's is not an expert in this yet, hence he can only do some specific transformation. Moreover, each transformation takes some time. See input format and explanation for a more in-depth discussion.

Everyone in the audience has their eyes on John Eddie which, to him, is a little bit frightening. In addition, John Eddie has to do this test times, each test having its own starting sculpture and duration of smoke bomb, .

Once the sculpture is revealed, the test immediately starts. How many distinct sculptures can John Eddie manage to show to the audience?

Explanation

The first test starts with Sculpture 2. John Eddie can activate a smoke bomb which gives him more than enough time to change it to Sculpture 1. After the audience sees Sculpture 1, John Eddie can activate a smoke bomb to change it back to Sculpture 2. After the audience sees Sculpture 2, John Eddie can activate one final smoke bomb which gives him just enough time to change it to Sculpture 3.

Hence, John Eddie can do three sculptures for the first query. Note that he can also do Sculpture 3 first instead of Sculpture 1 and the answer remains the same.

For the second test, John Eddie can only change Sculpture 3 to Sculpture 2. But he doesn't have enough time since the smoke bomb only lasts 3 seconds, one second less of the amount of the time he needs. And so, he can only present one sculpture to the audience - the original one (Sculpture 3).
