Mental health issues are taken seriously in space and so the most paranoid patients were taken into care in a special hospital to be guarded and cared for by robots.

The patients' cells are arranged in a row, with exactly one patient per cell. Each patient has a base paranoia rating, represented by a positive integer. This rating measures the agitation of the patient when exactly one robot is watching. If there are robots watching a patient, however, then their actual paranoia rating is times as much compared to if there was only one robot watching. The total paranoia of the people in the hospital is simply the sum of the actual paranoia ratings of all the patients in the hospital.

Even though the government does not wish the patients to become agitated further, the intergalactic space government must make sure that there is at least one robot watching each patient. At the disposal of the government are robots which can watch adjacent cells. Because of this, the robots will be assigned to watch a set of contiguous cells.

You are tasked to assign the robots to their respective cells. What is the least amount of total paranoia you can induce in the hospital?
