Sandara Park Park, a park named after Sandara Park is a rectangular park with dimensions square meters. It is divided into a grid of unit squares. Some of these unit squares have hedges that are impossible to pass through. Given a starting and ending location in Sandara Park Park, determine if it is possible to get from the starting location to the ending location whilst staying in the park premises at all times.

Note that you can only travel in the four cardinal directions. In other words, travelling diagonally is not allowed.
