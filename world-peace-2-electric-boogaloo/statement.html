And then it occurred to you that you must transform the row of carvings on the floor so it matches any one of those combinations on the wall!

Thankfully, you recently acquired a ring with a special power since one of Captain Planet's planeteers resigned. The power of the ring is described as follows: you point your ring to one of the symbols in the row of carvings and you imagine a symbol in your head. You shout Earth! and the symbol you were pointing to and all the other symbols in the row of carvings matching it become the symbol you were imagining. However, you realize that the puzzle is in itself a trap. This is because not all combinations on the wall will be attainable given the initial setup of the row of carvings. Will you be able to choose the correct combination in time? How many of those combinations will even let you out? That's for you to figure out!
