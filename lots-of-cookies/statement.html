Every Christmas, instead of giving pamasko to his inaanak, your Ninong always instead gives these Computer Science challenges, saying that he will only give pamasko to the deserving who are able to answer them. You, however, are a master programmer, so every year you answer all of his questions perfectly. When you ask him to pay up the Php P1600, though, it turns out he doesn't have any money, so he decides to make it up to you by buying you some cookies instead, along with a note that says, "Ninong is so proud of you!" Unfortunately, you don't actually like cookies that much, so you always end up giving them away to your friends anyway.

Suppose that on some year, you received cookies, and you want to know how Generous you can be with them (unlike your Ninong). So that none of your friends get jealous, you decide that if you would give cookies to some friend, they should get the same amount of cookies as every other friend.
