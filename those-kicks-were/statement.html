Kezra wants to join the Ninjas of Intellect, Prestige and Honor (NOIPH). The application process to NOIPH consists of a series of tests. Each test eliminates a number of applicants until they're left with the final set of NOIPH candidates.

Kezra wants to practice for the lattice test. This test is administered in the COTMWIASLNALSBIEOBDI, the Chamber Of The Mirror Which Is A Straight Line Not A Line Segment Because It Extends On Both Directions Indefinitely. It is named like that because it houses the Mirror Which Is A Straight Line Not A Line Segment Because It Extends On Both Directions Indefinitely, called MWIASLNALSBIEOBDI for short. And since the MWIASLNALSBIEOBDI extends on both directions indefinitely, then we should also assume that the COTMWIASLNALSBIEOBDI also extends on all directions indefinitely.

The test begins when a laser beam positioned at is fired towards the direction .

When this laser hits the MWIASLNALSBIEOBDI, dummies instantly appear in positions whose and -coordinates are both integers. (In mathematics, these are called lattice points). There is some exception though: Dummies will not appear on lattice points where the MWIASLNALSBIEOBDI passes through.

At the same time, the beam bounces off the MWIASLNALSBIEOBDI.

Now, once the dummies appear, Kezra must use his ninja quickness to go to the first dummy the laser beam is going to hit. He should then absorb the beam, thereby rescuing the dummy from total annihilation.

This is not only a test of agility, but also a test of wit! If the laser beam is not going to hit any of the dummies, then Kezra must not do anything and stay in his original position.
