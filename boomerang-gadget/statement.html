Graduates of the University of Skillful Technologies have developed a gadget that can heal a user or become a boomerang, depending on how it is set up. This group of diplomates believe that such a gadget will be useful during an intergalactic battle.

The gadget has LED lights, arranged in an grid. One can touch any of the LED lights on the machine to switch in from on to off or from off to on.

They test the gadget by sending it to important people disguised as an award, hoping that it was in healing mode. However, most of these gadgets kept returning to them as they apparently were set to boomerang mode! They were initially careless, but they are determined to figure out how to fix it.

A gadget is in healing mode if and only if there are the same number of lights that are turned on for every square on the grid.

When the gadget is activated, some set of LED lights turn on. It may or may not be in healing mode.

Given which lights are turned on and turned off in the beginning, what is the least number of LED lights must you touch in order to switch the gadget to healing mode?
