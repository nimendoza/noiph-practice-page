Drajded has just moved to a new country to study but he needs to get documents so that he wouldn't run into trouble with local authorities. These documents are a contract, a mobile phone number, a bank account, a residence permit, the list goes on!

He has to get a residence permit within his first three months from the consulate. However, to get a residence permit, he needs a proof of residence in a form of a contract from the residence agency, and proof of income from the institute providing his scholarship. To get his proof of income, he has to send the details of his local bank account to the institution giving him scholarship. To obtain a bank account, he needs to prove to the bank that he is a legal resident of the country in the form of a residence permit. He's already made calls in total, and each call gives him new information in the form of

To obtain document X from our office, one of the documents you have to submit is document Y.

And so, to get a residence permit, he needs to have a bank account and to have a bank account, he needs to have a residence permit. Now, he has already wasted a lot of time by calling up the offices and figuring out the prerequisites. Now, from the information that he has from the calls, he has to figure out if it is even possible to get all the things he needs to get without begging any of the offices to forego any of their prerequisites.
