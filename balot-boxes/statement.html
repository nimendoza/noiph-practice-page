There are boxes labelled to . The box contains fertilized duck eggs, also known as balot (plural is balots). These boxes are the entries of different duck farmers in the First Annual Balot Contest.

After submitting, they are left overnight and judged the next day. This practice unintentionally encourages some creative thinkers to mess with the balot boxes at night.

A lot of things can happen in the evening. They are classified into three different events.

COUNT SOBER

To make sure no funny business happens, an authorized balot watcher would occasionally count the total number of balots starting from the balot box to the balot box. A balot watcher counts this accurately all the time. The balot watcher writes the final count on his notepad.

COUNT DRUNK

An authorized drunk balot watcher would count the total number of balots starting from the balot box to the balot box. The drunk balot watcher has drunk a lot of taho and for some reason walks in a strange way. He would also count balots differently. For each box, he counts the first balot as balot. The second balot is counted as balots, and so on. For clarity, he counts the balot of every box as balots. Surprisingly, he never loses count and counts "correctly" in his way all the time. The drunk balot watcher writes the final count on his notepad.

HELLO GARCI

Among the participants, a newcomer named Penoy was predicted to be the winner. Some desperate participants did not like this. They would try to add more balots to their balot box.
