Dick likes big boxes and he cannot lie. This is something he cannot deny. On the other hand, his pet anaconda don't want none. One day, while Dick looked at his email's spam folder, he saw the subject MAKE YOURS BIGGER! COME TO BOX LAND!. Serendipitously, it turns out that Box Land was a magical land where you can bring a box and increase its size. Dick only had small boxes. This offer was a no-brainer for Dick and he immediately took the next bus ride to Box Land.

In Box Land, there were wizards and each can magically make your box bigger! To be more precise, the th wizard will be able to increase the length, width and height of your box by inches by casting a spell on it. Moreover, even though the spells of the wizards are unique, they may be able to increase the length, width and height of your box by the same number of inches.

Each wizard casts a different spell on the boxes and some spells are not compatible with each other. If one casts a spell on a box which was not compatible with the most recent spell that has been cast on it, it will explode. It took a lot of trial and error for the wizards but now, they know which pairs of spells are compatible and not. They also realized that if they cast a spell on the same box twice, it also explodes. So they don't do that too.

After a lot of testing, they discovered that there are pairs of spells which are compatible with each other. Moreover, save for the oldest wizard, each wizard's spell is compatible with the spell of at least one wizard older than them. However, the wizards are sensitive when it comes to the topic of age. We will respect their privacy and keep it a secret! All we can tell you is that none of the wizards were born at the exact same time.

Furthermore, the appearance of the box depends on the set of spells which has been cast on it. If the same set of spells have been cast on a box, then they will look exactly the same. Otherwise, they will look completely different.
