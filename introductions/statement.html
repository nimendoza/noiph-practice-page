In celebration for collecting enough stamps to get a planner from Stavucks, the popular coffee shop decides to throw a party for their loyal customers!

To keep things simple, we only consider the cases where if there are people in the party, there will be friendships. Each of the attendees will have at least one friend (i.e. they are part of at least one friendship). If you are unfamiliar with the concept of friendship, it means that two people are friends with each other.

For example, Desiree wants to be introduced to Savannah. Desiree can only talk to people she knows. Otherwise, things will be awkward. She can ask the people she knows to introduce her to any of their friends. Note that being introduced to someone means you get to know that person, but it doesn't imply you're friends.

One way to accomplish Desiree's mission of being introduced to Savannah is to ask her friend, Ophelia Mae, to introduce her to Xstacy. She should then ask Xstacy to introduce her to her friend Chastity, who is Savannah's friend. That way, Chastity could introduce Desiree to Savannah and they can finally be introduced. Note that only friends can introduce each other, and being introduced to one another doesn't make you friends.

If Ophelia Mae, Xstacy, Chastity are in the mood to introduce people, then Desiree would eventually be able to get introduced to Savannah. However, if any of the three suddenly become to arrogant and refuse to introduce friends to each other, then Desiree will not be able to get to know Savannah during the party. There is no other way she can get to know Savannah. If this is the case, then we say that Desiree and Savannah missed a connection.

Moreover, for the purposes of this problem, if everyone is cooperative, then there shouldn't be any missed connections. Hence, any missed connection is certainly caused by at least one person.

Now, for any person in the party, we would like to know how many pairs would miss a connection if that person (and only that person) would refuse to introduce anyone to any of her friends.
