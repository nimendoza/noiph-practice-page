The Cleaner Alien, an alien who cleans the mothership, is the only one left awake in the evening. There are different areas of the mothership and different hallways. Moreover, the hallways are set up such that it is possible to go from any area to any other by taking a sequence of (not necessarily distinct) hallways.

Every morning, trash is scattered in the hallways. In particular, exactly pieces of trash are scattered in the th hallway.

Every evening, The Cleaner Alien picks up the pieces of trash in such a way that there is a clean path from any area to another. A clean path is a sequence of hallways with no trash. The Cleaner Alien takes exactly one second to pick up one piece of trash.

If she fails to make sure that there is a clean path from any area to another, she gets fired and all the alien children she left in her home planet will not be able to finish alien college. She loves her alien children so much that she will not do anything to get fired from this job.

Due to her efficiency, The Cleaner Alien does the minimum amount of work. She chooses to clean a set of hallways such that she finishes cleaning as fast as possible every evening while still making sure she doesn't get fired.

Each of the original hallways have security cameras. The Illegal Alien, the only alien in the mothership capable of doing illegal things, tries to help one of the high-profile inmates escape. He has a two-phase plan in order to do so. Unlike the plans of this high-profile inmate, The Illegal Alien's plan is realistic and will take much less than six months.

Phase one of the plan is when The Illegal Alien makes new hallways, built one after the other, with no security cameras. Phase two is when he puts pieces of trash in them to repel The Cleaner Alien from changing her routine.

Each new hallway must connect two distinct areas which do not already have a hallway between them. Thus, at the end of phase one, no two distinct areas have more than one hallway between them.

The new hallways must be dirty enough such that The Cleaner Alien will not pass by in the evening and potentially catch escaping prisoners. In particular, The Cleaner Alien must still want to clean the original hallways despite the addition of the new ones. Note that if there exist a different set of hallways which take the same amount of time to clean, The Cleaner Alien will definitely choose that set of hallways for the sake of variety. Hence, The Illegal Alien must make sure not to introduce such a set by strategically putting pieces of trash in the new hallways.

The Illegal Alien only has a total pieces of trash to throw in the new hallways. In addition, he cannot add or remove new trash in the original hallways. Finally, he has no way of producing more pieces of trash.

Given , what is the maximum number of hallways The Illegal Alien can add such that The Cleaner Alien will still want to clean the original hallways? You must answer queries of this type.

Note that "pieces of trash" is always an integer. You can't have "half a piece of trash" because trash does not work that way. Even in outer space.
