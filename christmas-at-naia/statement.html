Personnel at the Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA) are very concerned with your safety. In fact, as a Christmas gift, they would happily put free bullets in your luggage just in case you need it for self defense while traveling.

Upon realizing that they were the lucky ones who received free bullets in their luggage, travelers would gleefully appreciate the gesture and send monetary donation to the staff. This is to encourage the honest thoughtful workers of NAIA to keep up the good work.

It's such a magical time of the year! Even the spokesperson for the police, claims that there is "no possibility" of these things to have happened in an airport. Truly, this is a magical phenomenon which can only be explained by the wonders of Christmas!

Unfortunately, the personnel only have a limited amount of bullets to share with the travelers and they must do this process incognito, so as not to appear they have some sort of favoritism.
