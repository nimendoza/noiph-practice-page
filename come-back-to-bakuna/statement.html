There are two types of districts -- residential and commercial. Residential districts have a specific demand of bakuna centers. These bakuna centers enable parents to vaccinate their children.

However, bakuna centers are not allowed to be constructed in residential districts! They can only be constructed in commercial ones.

Your goal is to choose where to construct bakuna centers to exactly meet the demands of each residential district. A residential district has a demand . This means that the residential district must exactly have neighboring districts with bakuna centers.

If there were less than neighboring commercial districts with bakuna centers, then there are not enough centers to vaccinate all the residents. If there were more than neighboring commercial districts with bakuna centers, then the residents would be paralyzed by so much choices and would not end up vaccinating their children. Hence, a residential district should have exactly commercial district neighbors with a bakuna center. If this is the case, then we say that the demand of residential district is met.

Your task is to help make sure that tigdas, polio, hepa, pneumonia, and meningitis are, in colloquial terms, "tsugi na". For simplicity, assume that there are no bakuna centers in Siyudad Sagana at this time. You must then choose which commercial districts should have a new bakuna center so that the demands of all the residential districts are met. If there are multiple ways of doing so, you need to minimize number of bakuna centers needed.

For each test case, you need to tell whether it is possible to construct new bakuna centers that satisfies all requirements, and if so, output at least one such configuration. The output for each test case would be a single line containing a single string which should be:

  • MAKAKAMISS if it is impossible to construct the bakuna centers that satisfies all requirements. In this case, makakamiss ang baby mo, misis.
  • a string of length if it is possible.

The string of length denotes the locations of the new bakuna centers, where the th character is:

  • B if district will contain a new bakuna center, and
  • K if district will not contain a new bakuna center.
